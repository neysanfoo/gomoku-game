What is Gomoku?

Gomoku, also known as Five in a Row, is a two-player strategy game played on a 15x15 board.

How to play

Gomoku is a strategy game played on a grid. The goal is to align five of your pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before your opponent.

Strategy

As with many strategy games, the key to winning Gomoku is to balance your offensive and defensive moves. It's important to watch your opponent's moves and try to anticipate their next move, while also making sure to protect your own pieces.
